The easier way to get around London

Ride anywhere Transport for London takes you with Google Pay – simply wake up your phone, tap and go.

Speed up your
journey with a tap

No need to dig for your wallet or wait in line to top up. Just wake up your phone, hold it to the reader, and go. Google Pay is accepted across all TfL services, from the London Underground to buses, trams, and beyond.

Save money with
TfL fare caps

Get daily and weekly fare caps when you use your phone to pay as you go with Google Pay. At the same price or less than a weekly ticket, it’s the cheapest way to travel around London – just remember to tap in and out with the same device each time.

Stay on top
of your trips

With rich receipts you can see everywhere you’ve been in one simple place. Just tap on any TfL transaction in the Google Pay app to check your journey and make sure you’ve paid the right fare.
